Marvel's Avengers and X-Men have had several memorable collaborations over the years, often resulting in epic crossovers that captivate fans. One of the most notable events is "Avengers vs. X-Men" (AvX), which took place in 2012. This storyline revolved around the return of the Phoenix Force, a cosmic entity of immense power. The Avengers and X-Men clashed over whether to use or destroy the Phoenix Force, leading to a massive battle that tested the limits of both teams2. Despite their differences, the Avengers and X-Men have also teamed up to face common threats, showcasing their combined strength and heroism. These collaborations highlight the rich history and dynamic relationships between Marvel's premier superhero teams.
